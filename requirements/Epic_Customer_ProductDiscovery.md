# Epic: Customer Product Discovery

## Business Context

This epic focuses on enabling customers to discover and explore athletic performance footwear products. As a new brand testing market demand, the product discovery experience must effectively showcase the unique value proposition of our athletic shoes to sports enthusiasts.

## Business Value

- Enable customers to find products that match their athletic needs
- Showcase product features and specifications that differentiate our brand
- Support market validation by tracking customer interest and engagement
- Provide essential product information to support purchase decisions

## Scope

**In Scope:**

- Product catalog browsing and filtering
- Product detail pages with specifications
- Product search functionality
- Category navigation for athletic shoe types

**Out of Scope:**

- Advanced recommendation engine
- Product comparison features
- Customer reviews and ratings
- Video content or 360Â° product views

## User Stories

### US001: Browse Product Catalog

**As a** customer  
**I want to** browse the available athletic shoe catalog  
**So that** I can see what products are available for my sport

**Acceptance Criteria:**

- Catalog displays all available athletic shoe products
- Products are organized by sport category (running, training, basketball, etc.)
- Each product shows key information: name, price, main image
- Pagination or infinite scroll for large catalogs
- Products can be sorted by price, newest, or popularity

### US002: View Product Details

**As a** customer  
**I want to** view detailed specifications for a specific shoe  
**So that** I can evaluate if it meets my performance needs

**Acceptance Criteria:**

- Product detail page includes multiple product images
- Detailed specifications: weight, cushioning type, support level, materials
- Available sizes and colors clearly displayed
- Price and stock status information
- Key performance benefits highlighted
- Size guide link for reference

### US003: Search for Products

**As a** customer  
**I want to** search for shoes by name or sport type  
**So that** I can quickly find relevant products

**Acceptance Criteria:**

- Search bar available on all catalog pages
- Search by product name, sport type, or key features
- Search results display relevant products with basic info
- Search filters for size, price range, and sport category
- "No results" state with helpful suggestions

### US004: Filter by Category

**As a** customer  
**I want to** filter products by athletic category  
**So that** I can find shoes specific to my sport

**Acceptance Criteria:**

- Category navigation: Running, Training, Basketball, CrossFit, etc.
- Filter by multiple categories simultaneously
- Clear indication of active filters
- Easy filter reset functionality
- Product count updates based on selected filters

## Business Rules

- Products must show accurate stock availability
- Pricing must be consistent across all product displays
- Product specifications must be accurate and comprehensive
- Out-of-stock products should be clearly marked but still visible
- New products should be highlighted appropriately

## Success Metrics

- Product page views per session
- Time spent on product detail pages
- Search usage rate and success rate
- Filter usage patterns
- Click-through rate from catalog to product details
